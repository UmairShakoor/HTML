Lighthouse

A hidden tool inside developer tools in your browser that helps you imporve both SEO & User Experience . How to open ; open your website in browser , right-click and click inspect(option) developer tools will appear , there is an option in devtools "lighthouse" . Click on it and analyze a report for your page . You will get score of different things including overall SEO etc. YOu will also get suggestions to improve SEO & User Experience.
